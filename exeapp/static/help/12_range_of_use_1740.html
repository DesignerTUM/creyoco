1.2 RANGE OF USE

In the context of university courses there are different areas of application.
A selection of options:

Before the course

Creating grading tests

  • Before the first lecture
  • Assessing the preexisting knowledge

Providing information about the course

  • Content overview of prior knowledge
  • Sources with hyperlinks
  • Embedded Wikipedia articles

Introduction to the seminar

  • Additional material is provided by the lecturers
  • Flipped Classroom â€“ preparation

During the lecture

Dealing with topics

  • Background knowledge / content-related additions in specific subjects
  • Embedding Wikis on relevant topics
  • Material can be used independently by the learners

Exercises and homework

  • Intermediate tests can be done by the learners to query their knowledge
  • Case studies can be presented in Creyoco

After the course

  • Contents of the learners can be added to supplement present Creyoco learning modules
  • Embedding in the learning platform as a standardised learning module
